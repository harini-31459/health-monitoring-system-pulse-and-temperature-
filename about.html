About Our Health Monitoring System

System Overview

The Health Monitoring System is designed to collect, analyze, and verify patient health information. It helps users understand their health condition by monitoring vital parameters and presenting meaningful health insights.

How the System Works

Users first log in securely, then enter patient details such as name, age, gender, contact, and medical history. The system allows users to input health parameters including blood pressure, heart rate, BMI, sleep count, and diabetes levels. Based on this data, the system analyzes and verifies the overall health status.

Key Features

✔ Secure login and user authentication
✔ Patient detail management
✔ Health parameter monitoring
✔ Disease awareness and verification
